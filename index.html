ICHI 2026

The 14th IEEE International Conference on Health Informatics

June 1–4, 2026 · Minneapolis, MN

Welcome to IEEE ICHI 2026!

ICHI 2026 is a premier community forum concerned with the application of computer science, information science, data science, and informatics principles, as well as information technology, and communication science and technology to address problems and support research in healthcare, medicine, life science, public health, and everyday wellness.


We invite you to share your research and insights at IEEE ICHI 2026. The conference highlights the most novel technical contributions to stakeholder-centered technology innovation for benefiting human health and the related social and ethical implications. ICHI 2026 will feature keynotes, a multi-track technical program including papers, posters, panels, workshops, tutorials, an industrial track, a doctoral consortium, and a women in healthcare informatics event.
