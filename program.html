Program Schedule

Overview

The ICHI 2026 program will include keynote talks, paper sessions, poster and demo presentations, tutorials, workshops, and industry panels.

More detailed information will be available in Spring 2026. Below is a tentative outline of the schedule.

Tentative Schedule

  • June 1, 2026 (Monday): Tutorials, Doctoral Consortium, Workshops
  • June 2–4, 2026: Main Conference (Keynotes, Papers, Posters, Demos)

Key Highlights

  • Plenary Keynotes from globally recognized experts in GenAI and Health Informatics
  • Parallel tracks: Analytics, Human Factors, Systems
  • Panel discussions on ethics, policy, and AI translation in clinical practice
  • Student poster competition and travel award session
