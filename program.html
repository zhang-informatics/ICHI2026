Program at a Glance

The tentative IEEE ICHI 2026 agenda includes workshops, keynote talks, scientific sessions, the doctoral consortium, young scholar programming, industry track panels, poster sessions, and the data challenge.

Below is a web-friendly version of the current agenda, organized by day and room for easier reading.

Tentative schedule. Final timing and room assignments may be updated.

Monday, June 1

Workshops, doctoral consortium activities, JHIR presentations, special sessions, and the opening keynote by Anita Burgun.

Tuesday, June 2

Young Scholar programming, industry panels, scientific sessions, poster session, reception, and the keynote by Hoifung Poon.

Wednesday, June 3

Parallel scientific sessions, the data challenge, the closing keynote by Li Shen, and conference wrap-up.
