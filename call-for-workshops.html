Call for Workshops

Submission Template

Overview

ICHI 2026 invites proposals for half-day and full-day workshops addressing timely topics in health informatics research, practice, or education. Workshops should facilitate interactive discussions and collaborative work among participants from academia, industry, and healthcare organizations.

Submission Guidelines

  • Workshop proposals should be 3–4 pages and include objectives, format, organizers, and target audience.
  • Clearly indicate whether the workshop is half-day or full-day.
  • Accepted workshops will be held on the first day of the conference (June 1, 2026).

Topics of Interest

  • Health AI applications
  • Wearable & remote patient monitoring
  • Privacy and fairness in health data
  • Multimodal health data integration
  • Human factors in digital health
  • Data-driven decision support

Important Dates

  • Proposal submission deadline: January 15, 2026
  • Notification of acceptance: February 15, 2026
  • Workshop day: June 1, 2026
