Call for Industry Track

Submission Template

Overview

The Industry Track at ICHI 2026 invites professionals and innovators from industry to share practical applications, case studies, and real-world solutions in the field of health informatics. This track fosters collaboration between academia and industry, and encourages submissions from startups, corporations, and nonprofit organizations.

Submission Guidelines

  • Industry papers may include case studies, lessons learned, or novel tools and technologies.
  • Papers must be 4–6 pages and follow IEEE formatting.
  • Accepted contributions will be presented in a dedicated industry session.

Topics of Interest

  • Health AI solutions in commercial settings
  • Deployment of clinical decision support tools
  • Regulatory and privacy concerns
  • Startups and innovation in health tech
  • Data pipelines and engineering for health systems
  • Industry-academic partnerships

Important Dates

  • Submission deadline: February 10, 2026
  • Notification of acceptance: March 20, 2026
  • Camera-ready version: April 15, 2026
  • Industry session: June 3, 2026
