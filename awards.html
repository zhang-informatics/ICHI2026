ICHI 2026 Student Travel Awards

The ICHI 2026 Student Travel Awards are sponsored by the Institute for Healthcare Informatics to support student participation in the IEEE International Conference on Healthcare Informatics. These awards recognize promising student researchers whose accepted work contributes to the advancement of healthcare informatics.

The awardees were selected by the ICHI 2026 Student Travel Award Selection Committee, co-chaired by Dr. Christopher C. Yang and Dr. Zhe He.
