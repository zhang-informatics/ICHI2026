Call for Tutorials

Submission Template

Overview

The Tutorial track of ICHI 2026 invites proposals for tutorials on cutting-edge topics in health informatics. Tutorials should be educational in nature and focus on emerging tools, technologies, or theories relevant to the field.

Submission Guidelines

  • Tutorial proposals should not exceed 3 pages, outlining objectives, structure, target audience, and presenter bios.
  • Submissions must follow the IEEE proposal format.
  • All selected tutorials will be scheduled for June 1, 2026 (the first day of the conference).

Suggested Topics

  • GenAI tools for healthcare
  • Multimodal health data processing
  • Wearable data analytics
  • Explainable AI and fairness in health models
  • Natural language processing for clinical applications
  • Open-source platforms in health research

Important Dates

  • Tutorial proposal deadline: January 20, 2026
  • Acceptance notification: February 20, 2026
  • Tutorial day: June 1, 2026
