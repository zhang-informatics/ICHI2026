Conference Venue

Explore the city of Minneapolis, MN — the home of ICHI 2026

Location

The IEEE ICHI 2026 will be held in Minneapolis, Minnesota, a vibrant city known for its cultural richness, scenic beauty, and innovative health ecosystem.

Conference Venue:

  • June 1 - 3: Coffman Memorial Union, University of Minnesota. 300 Washington Ave SE, Minneapolis, MN 55455, USA
  • June 4: McNamara Alumni Center, University of Minnesota. 200 SE Oak St, Minneapolis, MN 55455, USA

Getting to Minneapolis

Minneapolis is served by Minneapolis–Saint Paul International Airport (MSP), with easy access to downtown via light rail, taxi, and ride-share options. The venue is approximately 25 minutes from the airport.

Lodging

We have arranged special rates for ICHI 2026 attendees at nearby hotels. Details will be posted closer to the event. Hotels are within walking distance or a short transit ride to the venue.

  • Graduate Minneapolis (connected to McNamara Center)
  • The Commons Hotel
  • Days Hotel by Wyndham
  • Other hotels in downtown via light rail

About Minneapolis

Minneapolis is known for its beautiful parks, lakes, and cultural institutions such as the Guthrie Theater, Walker Art Center, and the Science Museum of Minnesota. Come early or stay longer to enjoy summer in the Twin Cities!
