1. Host Institutions

University of Minnesota and Mayo Clinic propose to co-host ICHI 2026 in Minneapolis on the University of Minnesota Twin Cities Campus.

2. Area Attractions

The University of Minnesota’s Twin Cities campus is located in a dynamic metro area rich with culture, nature, and dining.

3. Transportation

Minneapolis offers convenient transportation options including an extensive bus network and the METRO light rail system. The MSP airport is one of the top-rated airports in North America, offering direct flights from major cities across North America, Europe, and Asia. MSP is just a 10-minute drive from Minneapolis.

4. Conference Venue - Coffman Memorial Union

This building offers versatile and modern spaces equipped to accommodate a wide range of professional and academic gatherings. Located at the heart of the University of Minnesota campus, it is ideal for networking, collaboration, and knowledge exchange.

5. Plenary Sessions - Coffman Theater

Plenary sessions will be held in the Coffman Theater, a large auditorium with stadium-style seating, high-quality AV equipment, and lighting. Capacity: 401 attendees.

6. Breakout Sessions - Mississippi Room

Breakout sessions will take place in the Mississippi Room, which provides a flexible and professional environment for small group discussions and workshops.

7. Reception Location - Campus Club

Located on the 4th floor of Coffman, the Campus Club offers stunning views of downtown Minneapolis and the Mississippi River, in-house catering, a bar, and AV support. Capacity: up to 250 guests.
