Call for Doctoral Consortium

Submission Template

Overview

The Doctoral Consortium (DC) at ICHI 2026 is designed to support PhD students in the field of health informatics. The goal is to provide a venue for early-stage researchers to receive feedback, establish collaborations, and discuss their research with senior scholars and peers.

Submission Guidelines

  • DC submissions should include a 2-page abstract describing the student’s research topic, progress, and challenges.
  • Submissions must follow IEEE formatting guidelines.
  • Accepted students will present their work during a special DC session and receive mentorship.

Topics of Interest

  • Any area aligned with ICHI 2026 themes
  • Emerging methods and tools in health informatics
  • Work-in-progress research from PhD students

Important Dates

  • DC submission deadline: February 20, 2026
  • Notification of acceptance: March 25, 2026
  • Doctoral Consortium session: June 1, 2026
