About the Call

The 14th IEEE International Conference on Health Informatics (ICHI 2026) invites high-quality submissions for original research papers in the field of health informatics. We encourage work that bridges the gap between computing and medicine, demonstrating innovative technical approaches applied to real-world health challenges.

Topics of Interest

  • GenAI applications in healthcare
  • Machine learning and deep learning for health data
  • Natural language processing of clinical narratives
  • Health data integration and data fusion
  • Clinical decision support systems
  • Wearable and mobile health technologies
  • Health equity and informatics
  • Privacy, security, and ethics in health data

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit papers of up to 10 pages (including references) formatted using the IEEE double-column format. Submissions must be original and not under consideration elsewhere.

Important Dates

  • Paper submission deadline: January 31, 2026
  • Acceptance notification: March 31, 2026
  • Camera-ready submission: April 15, 2026
  • Conference dates: June 1–4, 2026
