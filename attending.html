Coffman Memorial Union

Coffman Memorial Union is a prominent event and meeting facility located at the heart of the University of Minnesota’s Twin Cities campus. The building offers versatile and modern spaces equipped to accommodate a wide range of professional and academic gatherings, including conferences, workshops, and seminars. With well-appointed meeting rooms, auditoriums, and flexible event areas, Coffman provides an ideal environment for networking, collaboration, and knowledge exchange. Its central location ensures easy access to campus amenities and public transportation, making it a convenient and attractive venue for regional, national, and international events.

McNamara Alumni Center

The McNamara Alumni Center is a 230,000 sq. foot office building and event center on the University of Minnesota Minneapolis campus. The center opened in 2000 and is privately owned by the University of Minnesota Foundation and University of Minnesota Alumni Association.
