Attending ICHI 2026

Registration Information

Details about registration categories, fees, and instructions will be posted in early 2026. Please check back soon!

Travel Information

ICHI 2026 will be held in Minneapolis, Minnesota, on the beautiful campus of the University of Minnesota.

  • Nearest Airport: Minneapolis–Saint Paul International Airport (MSP)
  • Transportation: Light rail service from airport to campus/hotels is available via Metro Green Line and Blue Line
  • Hotel Recommendations: A list of partnered hotels with conference rates will be published soon.

Visa Information

If you require a visa to attend the conference, please indicate this during registration. The organizing committee will provide official invitation letters upon request.

Student Support

We strongly encourage student participation and will offer a limited number of student travel awards. Application details will be released in Spring 2026.
