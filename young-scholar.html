Young Scholar Symposium

IEEE ICHI 2026 Featured Program

ICHI 2026 Highlight

A dedicated stage for emerging health informatics researchers.

The Young Scholar Symposium supports high school and undergraduate students pursuing research in biomedical informatics, health informatics, and AI for health. The program brings together lightning talks, posters, oral presentations, awards, and a panel discussion designed to help young researchers share work and connect with the ICHI community.
